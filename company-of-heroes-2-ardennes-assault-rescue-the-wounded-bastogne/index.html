COMPANY OF HEROES 2—ARDENNES ASSAULT—RESCUE THE WOUNDED (BASTOGNE)

[31 Oct 2021]

 

You’ll do this mission with Baker Co., unless Baker is not on your roster, in which case you’ll have to do it with Fox Co. This guide assumes Baker Co.

Offensive Phase

You start with two Shermans and one of the little M8 armored cars, plus two squads (cav rifle).

Immediately switch your Shermans to fire high-explosive shells and without delay send them and the M8 to the first point (point #1) on the road. The German infantry there cannot seriously harm any of your three vehicles so blast away, and have your infantry follow closely to capture the point and grab any ammo boxes lying around.

Repeat this, going up the map, for points 2, 3, and 4. To speed things up, have your tanks wipe some/most of the defenders at a given point, or even pass through making just one salvo, and then move on, leaving the survivors to be mopped up by your following infantry and the M8—and the call-in M5 with cav riflemen that will become available after a couple of minutes. Consider even having your tanks pass through point 1 without stopping, fire only a couple of salvos at point 2, and then go straight to point 4 while the rest of your force handles  points 1 and 2—and then 3 with the help of one of the tanks now coming back from point 4. Note that at the third and fourth points the Germans will have both Paks and very pesky AT-nading panzerfusiliers, so you will have to do lots of micro, your vehicles will sustain some damage, and you may have to spend a bit of time repairing them. Use the M8 in these firefights in a way that makes it less of a target, as it is far less robust than the Shermans. The Shermans can kill a Pak crew pretty easily—especially when working together—by running behind or beside the gun and hitting the crew with their main-gun HE rounds. Shermans can kill panzerfusiliers or other elite infantry by kiting but also just by coming directly up to them and blasting away with HE, then repairing the AT-nade damage later.

If you’re careful not to waste time, you will cap the upper point with more than two minutes left on the timer. I have done it with more than three minutes remaining. Getting the gold medal on this mission means winning the mission while also finishing this first phase quickly—the cutoff may be 0:00, and I have seen gold awarded for a 1:30 time, but anyway doing it before the timer hits 2:00 is pretty easy if you don’t dawdle.

Defensive Phase

You now have to defend the two middle points, points 2 and 3, against German attacks, which can come from either side. There will be three attacks in all. There will also be a side-mission.

At points 4 and 3, you can scrounge four AT guns as well as at least one HMG. Put all of those around point three, covering approaches from the left and (across the main road) the small road coming in from the right.

Position one of your Shermans to cover point 2, where space is cramped and crewed weapons cannot maneuver effectively—there are also pathing bugs that will cause AT gun crews here, in the presence of the enemy, to commit suicide in effect. As much as possible, leave room on the main road for passing ambulances.

One of your Shermans can rove around killing German units and racking up XP, and doing the side-mission—but only if it involves killing a crippled Panzer, killing a VIP, or rescuing allied squads. You can switch to AP rounds for tank-vs-tank action although HE rounds work pretty well against Pumas , Luchs, and scout cars, which are the only German armor you’ll see here.

You can try the gather-intel mission if you get that, but it requires scouring the map to find three items of “intel” and then bringing each of them back to base with a separate squad, all within just a few minutes, and you get no bonus unless you get all three items. It’s not reasonably doable, and frankly it’s one of many idiotic —and somehow never fixed—elements in COH2.

The attacks on points 2 and 3 will involve Pumas and Luchs tanks, scout cars, sturmpios, volksgrens, panzerfusiliers, and fallschirmjaeger squads—the latter fire automatic weapons (FG42s) and are very deadly even at longish range. You have artillery that can help with wiping German infantry. Note that the attacks usually will come from the side for which you are least prepared, i.e., the one opposite the side where you have all your weapons pointing.

Watch out for the icy ditch across the road from point 3. Somehow it is extremely deep and your tank will sink and be destroyed if it runs onto it and breaks through the ice.

When the defensive phase begins, you get an RE squad. If you can get it in place quickly and have spare manpower points, you can use it to build an FP/HMG between buildings covering the narrow road from the W to point 2.

You should finish this mission with 30-60 veterancy points, and zero or near-zero casualties—or even an increase in your force if you freed the allied squads and they joined your Co. as reinforcements.
