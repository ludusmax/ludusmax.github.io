COMPANY OF HEROES 2 — THEATER OF WAR — THE CRIMEA

Winnable sometimes but essentially broken

 

Theater of War Mission Pack: Operation Barbarossa

Mission: The Crimea

Difficulty Level: General

 

The Crimea mission is not reliably winnable on General level. You can win it occasionally by using HMGs, bunker-HMGs, Paks, mortars, a Panzer IV (Ausf J is available from some commanders), and any captured T-34 or KV-1, to secure the middle-VP, upper-VP, and routes from the VPs to your base, but it will never be anything other than a chance, technical win against an overwhelmingly superior opponent. Apart from the huge bias towards Russia in unit quality, quantity, and mobility (T-34s, KV-1s, T-70s, Katyushas), there is the problem—not limited to this mission—that the German CPU ally has defective coding. It doesn’t build enough units, it suicides what it does build, and so it collapses early, leaving the human player to face both Russian armies in the form of non-stop combined-arms attacks from all directions, especially along the narrow, very-hard-to-defend route from middle-VP to base.

On Captain level the mission is more reliably winnable using the same strategy described above, but still extremely frustrating to play—it always feels broken, given the collapse of the CPU ally, and the only reason the Russians don’t win is because they don’t go aggressively for your VPs.

You are not meant to play these missions 1 v 2, so any mission—like this one—with a routine CPU ally collapse is broken by definition. Play with a human partner if you have one; otherwise skip the Crimea.
