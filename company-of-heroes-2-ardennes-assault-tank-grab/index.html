COMPANY OF HEROES 2—ARDENNES ASSAULT—TANK GRAB

[9 Nov 2021]

 

Probably many would say this mission is so bad it spoils Ardennes Assault. I still find it hard to believe that a major gaming company put a product out the door that is this messed up, and then basically did zip when everyone complained. You trust these people to give you value for money when they do COH3?

Victory in “Tank Grab” is won not by holding territory or even by killing German units, but by the completely unreal achievement of killing German units with your vehicles only. I guess it’s the kind of thing you end up with if you decide to depart from simulating war and instead construct a highly artificial, rule-bound, sport-like contest.

Anyway, in principle you win “Tank Grab” by refueling/repairing/recrewing lots of vehicles that are lying around the map, and using these captured vehicles to kill German vehicles—and the more plentiful German infantry. Points are deducted from the German score every time you make a kill.

In practice, the mission is a complete fubar. If you start to control the map (especially on levels 1 and 2) the AI deprives you of targets so that you have to base-rush to end the mission—and then you’ll be penalized with bronze for doing that. On difficulty level 3, you’ll swiftly face the scariest German armor (e.g., Tiger Ausf B, Tiger Ace, Sturmtiger, Panther) as well as extremely AT-capable German infantry, even though the mission briefing states that the Germans will have only “light mechanized support.” Levels 4 and 5 are worse, of course, mainly due to swarms of elite infantry.

Being able to repair abandoned vehicles quickly is essential in this mission, yet your RE squads and vehicle crews appear to be nerfed in that capacity—any significant repair job on a tank will take minutes, i.e., a large fraction of the total mission time, unless you can put multiple RE squads onto it.

There are also significant bugs: You may refuel/recrew some big tank only to find that it doesn’t fire. And the German point total does not keep proper track of your vehicles’ killing sprees—i.e., for some of your vehicles, KIAs bring no change to the German point total.

Finally, there is a problem that makes me wonder if this whole mission with all its imbalance against the player is not “broken” through incompetence but instead is a deliberate act of malice by the devs, possibly in the same vein as Faceoff at Rostov. On some maps (e.g., Lierneux) and maybe all of them, German-captured Stuarts are indestructible. They will take damage up to 95% or so, including main-gun-disabled, but can always continue firing their MG and can’t ever be killed by anything. Also, I notice that the Germans in this mission seem to capture these Stuarts first, before going for larger, more powerful vehicles. How is that a bug and not intentional coding? Certainly it ruins whatever mission it appears in, creating an artificial penalty for the player in his campaign.

“Medals will be awarded based on how quickly you win the mission” is the condition displayed at the start, but is basically false. You can win quickly, in fact most quickly, with a base-rush, but, again, if you do that you’ll be penalized with a bronze medal. I have won gold on this mission with Baker and Dog Cos. on level 1 or level 2 maps, but it cost them a lot of casualties. Rangers are a natural choice for this but they seem to be nerfed in the scoring so that they cannot win gold on this mission, ever—a swift, flawless mission for them will win only silver, and anything less than that will win bronze.

My advice if you’re going to play this mission is just to use Rangers and do a base-rush asap, to get it over with. Fuck you, Relic.
