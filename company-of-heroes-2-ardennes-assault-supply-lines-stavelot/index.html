COMPANY OF HEROES 2—ARDENNES ASSAULT—SUPPLY LINES (STAVELOT)

[9 Nov 2021]

 

The object here is to stop fuel trucks from leaving a depot and then capture the depot and hold it for a few minutes against furious counterattacks.

It’s a moderately difficult mission if you want to get the gold medal. Gold requires killing all fuel trucks but one, i.e., you may let the first one go but you must kill all the others. (It is possible to stop even the first one, but that cannot be done every time.)

To achieve this, you have to move immediately north with your starting units, or anyway within the first minute, and defeat the volksgren/panzerfusilier squads at the nearest point to cap it. (Use your starting RE squad to take a munitions cache about 60 m NNW of your base, and then take a similar cache at the next point.)

You must then move without delaying more than another minute or so to the next point, where you will deal with a panzerfusilier squad, a fallschirmjaeger squad, a Luchs light tank, and sometimes a 75mm infantry support weapon—not easy. If your only vehicle is an M8 scout car, send it along the road past the second point to the bottom of the bridge and then in the path of the next fuel truck, while your infantry distract/fight the German units at that second point. You need to get some AT power (the scout car is v. weak but will do temporarily) in the way of the fuel truck. If you have Rangers, your Ranger units can also try to fight-and-run along the road past the second point; if un-upgraded they can lay demo charges in the paths of a few more fuel trucks before you bring in better AT power.

Don’t go north of the river, as there are too many other units there.

You can see the path of the fuel trucks on the map. There are a few possible routes, and each mission playthrough uses one of them.

If you ran through the second point, you’ll have to build up a solid anti-infantry and anti-tank force at the first point, and then attack to take the second point. HMGs, AT guns, mortars, LMG-infantry, and AT-equipped Rangers are all good.

When the third truck comes along, it will be guarded by a Luchs or a Puma or a flak-HT, and you’ll have to deal with both vehicles. Obviously, you will need to build up your forces on the fuel truck path to handle this task.

Once you have taken the second point and have an adequate force on the fuel truck path to keep snuffing those trucks indefinitely, build fuel caches on the two points you’ve taken and bring in a couple of Shermans, and then a 75mm self-propelled gun or other mobile artillery, or at least a mortar team or two. Capture any German infantry support gun if you can.

You can slowly move your force toward the truck depot. When you get to the point nearest the depot, the trucks will leave a bit more often, now always with a Luchs or Puma escort.

Remember that the Germans have tons of firepower and veteran, hard-to-kill units. Let your tanks and artillery do most of the work, to keep your casualties down.

Eventually, when you get bored of racking up XP, move on the truck depot, use artillery/mortars on the HMG (at the back), Pak, elite infantry, and maybe infantry support gun guarding the depot point, then put LMG/HMG and schreck/bazooka-equipped squads in buildings around the depot point, using your tanks and AT gun to deal with German units coming from off the map. Take the point, and hold it for a couple of minutes against infantry + Luchs/Puma attacks.
