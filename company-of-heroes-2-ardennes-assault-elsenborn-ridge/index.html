COMPANY OF HEROES 2—ARDENNES ASSAULT—HOLD THE LINE (ELSENBORN RIDGE)

[26 Oct 2021]

 

“Hold the Line” is the first regular mission in the Ardennes Assault campaign. It is complex and the German AI is programmed to be versatile—to me, no other mission is as unpredictable.

If Dog. Co. is one of your active companies on the campaign, you’ll be forced to play this mission with them. If not, you’ll play with Rangers. It’s better to play it with Dog Co.—key units like HMGs and AT guns are cheaper for them, and they have much better artillery.

To win this mission, you must keep the Germans from capping either of the two VPs behind your front fortifications. To win gold, you must win without resorting to the mission-special call-in artillery (you can use Dog. Co artillery and your major’s call-in artillery without penalty). Both of those goals are easy to accomplish. The challenge here is keeping casualties down while accumulating a reasonable number of experience points.

Here’s my favorite way to play this mission, using Dog Co. It should allow you to minimize casualties and hassle, while usually achieving 40-60 XP, with a decent chance of nailing a veterancy-bonus side-mission if you choose.

Preparation phase

In the ~3-minute period before the German attack starts (there is a countdown timer), bring in the lieutenant and an HMG.

Your starting RE squads should immediately lay tank traps (TTs) between your forward fortifications and between the fortifications and the woods to either side (see images), in order to block German vehicles coming from the top of the map. Do not leave gaps, and do not use pre-existing TTs and obstacles as part of your line—those objects are not intact and German vehicles will eventually break through them. Your assault engineers, and an RE squad when available, should lay wire on the left and right sides of the line, on the friendly side of the TTs. Note that Ardennes Assault, like other COH2 elements, has pathing bugs—your TT- and wire-laying squads will need some micro to get most quickly where they need to be.

Upgrade your two front-center firing positions (FPs) with HMGs as the necessary munitions points become available. Place your starting HMG and the first new one you bring in behind sandbags where they can cover the far left and far right sides of your main line (i.e., the parts not covered by your front-center FPs). Your rifleman squads and the Lt can defend from behind the front fortifications. Do not place your RE squads in any of the FPs—the grenades they throw will damage your lines of TTs, allowing German armor breakthroughs.

20:00 to 18:00

Build an FP (upgrade w/HMG asap) and bring in an AT gun to cover the W road through the woods. The map will open up to the W at 18:00 and Germans will soon start to assault down that road.

Note: If you don’t want to deal with German vehicles or attempt a side-mission, just build TTs across the mouth of that W road, leave the upgraded FP unmanned, place your AT gun elsewhere, and focus on defending your main line for the rest of the mission. The suggestions below assume that you will keep the road open.

However, you should know that side-mission #1, taking the checkpoint up the W road, is worse than useless; it will cost casualties and will result in the arrival of Easy Co., whose firefights with German armor on your main line will break your TTs. Side-mission #2 (random selection of tank-kill, VIP-kill, intel-gather, or allies-save) will gain you at most an additional 5 or 6 experience points, while it could cost you a lot of casualties—and the intel-gather and allies-save missions are not reasonably achievable.

18:00 to 14:00

Dismiss your riflemen and one of your three RE squads. Bring in more AT guns to cover the W road until you have a total of eight. Set them to ignore infantry.

On your main line, build a new FP about 15m back from the pre-existing center ones. Upgrade the pre-existing far-left and far-right FPs when you can spare the munitions points; when you do that, bring the mobile HMG that was covering the same area into the center (or wherever else German infantry is concentrating). In general, from this point on, build FPs and place HMGs where they are most needed—but don’t build FPs until you have the munitions points to upgrade. Use your RE squads to build sandbag walls for your HMGs in front of the second line of fortifications, where you will soon fall back. When the Germans drop smoke in front of the main line at 14:00, pull back your squads and HMGs to those sandbagged positions just in front of the second line of fortifications. German vehicles will be able to kill all your front FPs but shouldn’t be able to reach your new FPs and weapons crews positioned farther back.

14:00 to ~5:00

You can now expect significant German armor attacks (from the W road) at any time. (Ignore German vehicles on your main line unless they somehow break through your TTs.) Note that some German vehicles will move along the main road in front of your main defense line and disappear to the left; they are on their way to attack down the W road.

Dismiss another of your RE squads to free up manpower points.

If the Germans start sending all their infantry down the W road, not just their vehicles, then you’re in luck and can maximize XP on this mission—but you will need to adjust your defenses by bringing your HMGs over, building another one or two FPs to cover the W road, manning those FPs to toss grenades, switching some of your AT guns to fire at infantry, etc. Soon your Lt. unit should be able to pick up an LMG dropped by German elite infantry; that will help a lot.

On your main defense line, build more FPs (set back at the new positions) as needed to deal with German infantry.

Bring in a major, put him in your second line of fortifications near the W road, and set him as a retreat point.

If you want to attempt the side mission (kill VIP or kill tank), prepare to bring in a Sherman tank as soon as you have the fuel. If the side mission is rescue-allies or gather-intel, plan to skip it and stay focused on defense. The side-mission is announced at around the 8:45 mark.

~5:00 to end

Bring in the Sherman, if you’ll be attempting the side-mission or if you just want to use it for defense (it is very good against bunched infantry, when using HE rounds). You can alternatively bring in (sooner) the M8A1 75mm howitzer which is also good for killing German infantry, but it is weak to any German vehicle, even a scout car, so should stay in your main base area, in a defensive role. Don’t use either the M8 or the Sherman where they will damage your TTs, unless the countdown is below two minutes.

For the side-mission, poke cautiously with the Sherman and kill the German infantry on the W road in the woods—or, if time is short, try running as quickly as possible up the W road, through the checkpoint, and towards the village to attempt the side-mission. (Avoid capturing the checkpoint, as noted above.) The kill-VP and kill-tank missions (use AT artillery for the latter) are easy. The save-allies mission should not be attempted because it has to be done too early in the mission. The gather-intel mission takes way too much time and usually is way too risky.

When done with the side-mission, bring your tank back to your main defense line—or keep it out of sight in the village if going back would be too hazardous. Use your AT guns to kill any more German armor that comes down the W road.

That’s it. You should finish with few if any casualties, and most of the time you should have a healthy veterancy points score in the 40-70 range.

An alternative strategy is to defend the main line as described here, but put TTs across the W road before the 18:00 mark, do not bring in any AT guns, and use the Lt, Cptn, an HMG, a mortar team, and several rifle squads to clear out the W road and attempt the side-mission (while not taking the checkpoint), starting around the 10:00 mark. Attempting the side-mission is risky with only a small potential reward, though.

Another alternative, which is more reliable at building XP—and more “kinetic”—but likely to be more costly in terms of casualties, is to TT the main line and the W road, ignore all side-missions, and bring in 10-12 AT guns, keeping them pointed away from the main line until about the 5:00 mark. At that point, bring them forward to start nailing the accumulated German armor outside the TTs.
