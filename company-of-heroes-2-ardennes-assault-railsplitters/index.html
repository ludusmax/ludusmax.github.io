COMPANY OF HEROES 2—ARDENNES ASSAULT—RAILSPLITTERS

[4 Dec 2021]

 

The mission here is not to hold VPs or drive German victory points down, but simply to destroy 5 German command trucks. You win gold by destroying the command trucks within some unspecified time period. I almost always have won gold, so it’s not that hard. Just remember to stay focused on the main goal of finding/killing those command trucks.

On levels 1-2 you’ll find at least three fixed/sandbagged trucks on the main map. Two are weaponless and unguarded. The third has a flak gun with a long range and strong effect against infantry even in buildings far away; if one of your squads stumbles onto it, they should get out of the flak gun crew’s sight or just retreat to base. (Ignore the stupid UI voice-over suggestion to “try a pincer attack.”) There is also another, mobile truck, usually parked near the outside of the German base with an elite infantry guard (on level 3 and up this is a second flak halftrack), and the last truck is the main base building (and sometimes just outside the entrance, in level one missions).

You’ll start with a 75mm pack howitzer, HMG, rifle squad, and RE squad. Use them initially to cap points to get resources flowing, especially fuel. You want to bring in a captain asap, and then a mortar team, and then the Stuart, and then more infantry with good equipment (riflemen and REs with BARs/bazookas, cav riflemen, Rangers, paras with LMGs), plus another Stuart. It’s risky to go for a Sherman because it takes so long, but on higher difficulty levels it might be your best bet.

The locations of the command trucks are the same on each map, so experience helps. When you find the flak-truck, use the pack howitzer on it and follow with the mortar when you have that.

When you’ve destroyed the first three fixed trucks and the mobile truck, you’ll have to approach the German base, destroy one or two flak emplacements at the entrance, wipe any Pak or raketenwerfer crews that stand in your way, and then take out the base building. It’s usually not that hard, but even on level 1 difficulty the Germans typically will bring out a King Tiger after a while, so you’ll need to have the AT power to deal with that as well.

On level 3 or 4 or 5, also watch out for multiple elite German infantry squads and strafing Stukas.
